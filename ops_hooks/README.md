# OPS Hooks

OPS apporte l'attestation et la gouvernance operationnelle apres la decision locale.

## Fonctions attendues

- journalisation des decisions
- attestation des executions
- conservation des receipts
- regles d'usage des secrets
- preparation au replay et au diff

## Evenements minimum

- bundle pulled
- manifest verified
- signature verified
- policy denied or allowed
- payload executed
- receipt written
- secret resolved
